Title                   : Blood Machine
Filename                : BloodMachine.wad
Release date            : 06/10/2022
Author                  : Whark
Email Address           : c7ein37q@duck.com

Description             : A small, Doom II map focused on atmosphere and
                          tight, concise encounters.

                          Jumping, crouching, freelook, sprinting, gameplay mods
                          welcome and encouraged but not required.

Your Objective
                          Deal with the turned crew of Rig 31, cleanse the
                          bowels of the machine, shut down the blood machine.
